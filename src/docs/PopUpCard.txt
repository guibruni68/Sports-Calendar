POPUP CARD
==========
Figma node: 1:6258
Component set: "PopUp Card"

DESCRIPTION
-----------
Large featured match card (600 × 441px) with decorative geometric mesh
background, championship title, two team logos with names, date/time or
AO VIVO badge, optional tag jogo, and broadcast channel row.
