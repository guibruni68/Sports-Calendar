CARD DESTAQUE
=============
Figma node: 1:6436
Component set: "Card destaque"

DESCRIPTION
-----------
Featured/highlight card component with 3 display types (tipos):
- Jogo: Match card with two team logos, date/time pill or "AO VIVO" badge, and broadcast channels
- Time: Team card with logo and team name
- Competição: Competition card with logo and competition name

VARIANTS
--------
Properties:
  - Tipo: "Jogo" | "Time" | "Competição"
  - Ao vivo: True | False (Jogo only — show live badge vs. date pill)
  - Hover: True | False
  - Game Date: TEXT (default "27/01 19:00")

Total: 8 Figma variants (3 tipos × hover states × ao vivo states)
